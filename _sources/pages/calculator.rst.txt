Scientific Calculator & Unit Converter
***************************************

========    ===================
Personal    Feb 2020 - Apr 2020
========    ===================

``HTML``  ``CSS``  ``JavaScript``  ``Chrome APIs``  ``GIMP``

A simple scientific calculator & unit converter which provides instant results as you type; detects and warns of wrong angle unit choice; generates shortcuts for the most used conversions; and supports dark mode. It is available as a web application and browser extension and garnered over 2000 users.
